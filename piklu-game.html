📖 How to Play PIKLU

🎯 Objective

The objective is simple - find the spy! Regular players know the secret location and must identify who among them is the spy. The spy doesn't know the location and must deduce what it is while remaining undetected.

👥 Player Setup

  • 3-4 Players: 1 Spy + 2-3 Citizens
  • 5-6 Players: 2 Spies + 3-4 Citizens
  • 7-8 Players: 3 Spies + 4-5 Citizens
  • 9+ Players: 3 Spies + Rest are Citizens

🎮 Game Play

Step 1: Everyone joins and gets their secret card. They see the location EXCEPT the spy who sees the SPY card instead.

Step 2: Everyone goes to Discord call. Players take turns asking each other questions (via voice chat) to identify the spy.

Step 3: The spy must listen carefully and try to figure out the location while pretending to know it.

Step 4: After discussion, everyone votes to eliminate someone they think is the spy.

Step 5: The player with the most votes is eliminated and revealed.

🏆 Winning Conditions

  • Citizens Win: If they successfully identify and eliminate all the spies
  • Spies Win: If they remain hidden until the end or get enough votes against non-spies
  • Spy Wins Alone: If only one spy remains and majority of players are eliminated

💡 Tips & Strategy

  • Ask specific questions about the location to catch the spy
  • If you're the spy, ask general questions that could apply to any location
  • Pay attention to how people answer - inconsistencies reveal the spy!
  • Build alliances with trusted players
  • The spy might pretend to know the location - listen for suspicious hints!

⏱️ Game Duration

A typical game lasts 8-12 minutes, including setup time.
